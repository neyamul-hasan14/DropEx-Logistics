How to Do Business with DropEx

Welcome to DropEx, where we redefine global shipping and logistics. Partnering with us is simple and ensures a seamless delivery experience. Follow the steps below to start working with us:

Step 1: Register Your Business

  • Visit our website at www.dropex.com and click on Register.
  • Fill out the business account registration form with your company details.
  • Submit the necessary documents (business license, tax ID, etc.).
  • Receive a confirmation email with your unique business ID.

Step 2: Select Your Service Plan

  • Express Delivery: Fast and reliable for urgent shipments.
  • Standard Delivery: Cost-effective and timely shipping.
  • Custom Freight: Tailored solutions for bulk cargo.
  • Contact your dedicated account manager for guidance.

Step 3: Set Up Logistics

  • Access our user-friendly platform to manage your shipments.
  • Schedule pickups, track deliveries, and monitor logistics in real-time.
  • Integrate our API for automated shipment management.

Step 4: Start Shipping

  • Prepare your packages using our shipping guidelines.
  • Print shipping labels from your dashboard.
  • Drop off shipments at the nearest DropEx branch or request a pickup.

Step 5: Grow with Us

  • Benefit from discounted rates as your shipping volume increases.
  • Access analytics and insights to optimize your supply chain.
  • Partner with DropEx for marketing and branding opportunities.
